This page is a collection of the art I was commissioned to make for the Dunlap High School Drumline! The hoodie designs were based on the theme of their performances.

2021 Hoodie

The title of this year's performance was called "Per Aspera Ad Astra." All of the artwork made for this hoodie was done on my phone using a small stylus on an app called "Sketchbook."

2022 Hoodie

The theme of this year's performance was titled: "Uninvited." For this hoodie, I used Procreate on my Ipad to create the design.

Meat at the Strip Poster

This was a promotional poster I made in Photoshop for a sponsored car event. I was only given the logo for the sponsor, a list of cars to display, and a general idea. I recreated the logo so that it would be less pixelated, adjusted the colors of the cars, added shadows, and add smoke to come from the plane flying. There are two versions shown here.
